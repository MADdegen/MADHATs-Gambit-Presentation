Users commit bet hash pre-resolution, reveal post-oracle. Eliminates front-running and latency arbitrage completely.
DISPUTE WINDOW
Strictly 48 hours for ambiguous cases. Evidence must be timestamped/archived. Bad-faith disputes result in stake slashing.

Launch markets use objective, machine-readable data only. Subjective markets introduced after 6-month proven oracle accuracy.
